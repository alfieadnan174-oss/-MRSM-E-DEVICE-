About This Project

MRSM E DEVICE (Mutual Resource & Skill Matcher – Empowerment DEVICE) is a digital platform designed to visualize and redistribute community support.

The system makes invisible care work measurable, highlights imbalances in giving and receiving support, and connects neighbourhood needs with local capacity.

🌍 SDG 11 – Sustainable Cities and Communities

Promoting resilient and supportive neighbourhood ecosystems. Developed as part of the SEM enrichment initiative — demonstrating how technology can strengthen community coordination without monetary exchange.
