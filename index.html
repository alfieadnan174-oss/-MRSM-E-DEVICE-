MRSM E-DEVICE
A Web-Based Community Support Visualization Platform
01
Problem Statement & Objectives
/5

Modern communities rely on informal support networks — caregiving, emotional assistance, and voluntary task-sharing. However, this work remains invisible and untracked, resulting in imbalanced participation, burnout, and inequality. No structured digital platform currently exists to coordinate or visualize these community efforts.

  • Develop a web-based platform to record and categorize community support activities
  • Visualize the give-and-receive balance to expose participation imbalances
  • Centralize community needs into a structured, searchable system without monetary exchange
  • Promote equitable redistribution of neighborhood support aligned with SDG 11 — Sustainable Cities and Communities
